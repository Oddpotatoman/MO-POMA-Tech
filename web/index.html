ABOUT MO-POMA

We as students at University of Agder created a group named MO-POMA that is an acronym stands for the first letter of each name member. We are glad to be called as the most international group that may encourage us to work better. In this semester, we are working on developing a better functionality of the existing Learning Management System (LMS) through three subjects IS-200, IS-201 and IS-202. The overall purpose of the project is to make an alternative platform to "Canvas", this one will contain new features and functionalities. Thus, both teachers and students would be able to use the platform easily and effectively.
